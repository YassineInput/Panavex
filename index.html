01

What types of businesses can use Panavex payment processing?

Panavex is designed for restaurants, retail stores, e-commerce businesses, service providers, or any business that needs reliable payment processing. We support businesses of all sizes, from single locations to multi-state operations with 6,000+ accounts.

02

Do you support mobile and in-person payments?

Yes, our payment processing works seamlessly across all channels - mobile payments, in-person POS terminals, online transactions, and contactless payments. Our system is fully responsive and works on any device.

03

Do I need technical expertise to set up payment processing?

No coding required. Our team handles the complete setup process including POS terminal configuration, payment gateway integration, and staff training. You can start processing payments within 48-72 hours.

04

Do you provide ongoing support and system updates?

Yes, all clients receive 24/7 technical support, automatic system updates, security patches, and compliance updates. Our support team includes payment processing specialists who understand your business needs.

05

What are your processing fees and contract terms?

We offer competitive processing rates starting at 2.5% + $0.30 per transaction for qualified businesses. No long-term contracts required. Rates may decrease based on processing volume. Contact us for a custom quote based on your business needs.

06

What happens if I have payment processing issues?

Our 24/7 technical support team resolves 95% of issues within 2 hours. We provide phone, email, and live chat support. For critical issues affecting transactions, we offer immediate escalation to our senior technical team.
